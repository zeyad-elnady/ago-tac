Our History
and Values

AgoTac was established in 2009, joining the lead of industrial supply companies in Egypt and the Middle East by providing differential products and services, gaining customers' respect and satisfying their needs. We ensure a fair profit for our employee-owners and stakeholders by embracing sound, ethical business practices.
Within just two years, AgoTac became known as the premium straw quality manufacturer with the best prices.
  • Market Share 2022: 40% Egypt, 60% Export
  • Production Capacity: 780M straws yearly
  • 2023 Target: 10% capacity increase

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the preferred partner for comprehensive packaging solutions in the MENA region, committed to quality and innovation.
Mission: To market and provide a service that ensures customer satisfaction, ease of communication, improved working processes, and cost savings, all while maintaining a total-quality environment.
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Quality Environment
  • Economic Support
  • Continuous Innovation
